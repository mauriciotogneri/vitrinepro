A small organic grocery & health-food shop ("magasin diététique & santé bio") in the lively **Jonction** quarter of Geneva, run under the brand **BioBoulevard by VivaVeg**. It sells organic and local foods, a broad vegan range (tofu, flours, kombucha, kéfir), gluten/lactose/sugar-free products, food supplements and natural beauty products, with a zero-waste focus — and hosts in-store tastings and wellness conferences.
