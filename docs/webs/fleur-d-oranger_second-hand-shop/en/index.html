The boutique

An atmosphere that invites you to travel.

In the heart of La Jonction, on Boulevard Carl-Vogt, Fleur d'Oranger is a second-hand and consignment boutique run by Aamir Ali.

Here you'll hunt for clothing, bags, jewellery and watches in a warm, carefully arranged setting. Every piece is chosen and displayed with care — and you're welcomed with the time to listen and advise, never to pressure.

“The oriental atmosphere makes you want to travel.” — Renée Delajoux, customer
