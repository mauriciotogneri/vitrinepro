Our story

A family cantina, since 2018

Le Mexicain opened in La Jonction in 2018 — a small, family-run address serving generous, authentic Mexican cooking in a prettily decorated room that opens onto a garden terrace.

Real corn tortillas, dishes made from scratch: in just a few years it has become one of Geneva’s best-loved Mexican tables — #209 of 1,882 restaurants and a Travelers’ Choice award on TripAdvisor, 500+ reviews and a 4.8 rating on Google.

In the kitchen and front of house, a team of just a few — and an owner whom reviewers single out for the welcome.
