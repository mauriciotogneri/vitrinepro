The house

An Italian table in the Jonction

Il Colosseo is a family affair on Boulevard de Saint-Georges, a stone's throw from Plainpalais. People come for simple, careful Italian cooking: wood-fired pizza, fresh pasta and a warm welcome.

Wood fire, fresh produce, and the time it takes. Nothing more.

At the oven, the dough proves slowly before a few minutes in the flames. From the cellar, an Italian wine list and the house limoncello round off the meal.

Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to book?

Booking is recommended, especially in the evening and at weekends. Call us on +41 22 320 39 63.

Do you offer takeaway?

Yes — most of our pizze and dishes are available to take away.

Do you have vegetarian options?

Yes, several pizze and pasta dishes are vegetarian. Just ask and we'll guide you.

Which payment methods do you accept?

Credit cards and cash are accepted. Gift cards are also available.

Is there a terrace?

Yes, we have seating on the terrace and inside.

Is the restaurant wheelchair accessible?

For any accessibility questions, please contact us — we'll gladly tell you about access and seating.
