our little corner

A small café with a big welcome

Yummy Coffee sits quietly on Rue Dancet, a couple of minutes from the Plaine de Plainpalais and just down the road from Uni Mail. It's the kind of place where the barista learns your order, the croissants come out warm, and a quiet "bonjour" greets you on the way in.

We keep things simple and made-with-care: proper coffee, an almond-milk chai people travel for, and a little counter of bakes and breakfast prepared fresh each morning. Nothing fussy — just good things, done well, in a room that feels like it's yours.

  • Specialty coffee & almond-milk chai, made to order
  • Homemade croissants, apple cake, brownies & sandwiches
  • Sit-in or takeaway — and kind on the wallet (1–10 CHF)

what we're loved for

Three good reasons to drop by

Carefully brewed coffee

From a quick morning espresso to a flat white worth slowing down for — plus the almond-milk chai latte our regulars keep coming back for.

Homemade bakes

All-butter croissants, fudgy brownies and that famous apple cake — baked in-house and best while they're still warm from the morning.

A proper breakfast

Open from 7:30 with everything from a smoked-salmon sandwich to granola and fresh juice — the gentlest possible start to a Geneva morning.
