The shop

A neighbourhood boutique, since 2012

Le Panapé de Caméla opened its doors in 2012 on Rue du Général-Dufour, a stone's throw from the centre of Geneva. The shop became Le Panapé de Caméla Sàrl in 2015, but the spirit hasn't changed: a small, light-filled place where every shelf holds a surprise.

People come for a gift that stands out, a lovely piece of ceramic, a soft throw or a toy that brings back childhood. Handmade objects sit alongside retro finds, in a cheerful, colourful setting.

And above all, they come for the welcome — the thing your reviews mention most.
