The shop

A neighbourhood boutique, since 2012

2012the year we opened

Le Panapé de Caméla opened its doors in 2012 on Rue du Général-Dufour, a stone's throw from the centre of Geneva. The shop became Le Panapé de Caméla Sàrl in 2015, but the spirit hasn't changed: a small, light-filled place where every shelf holds a surprise.

People come for a gift that stands out, a lovely piece of ceramic, a soft throw or a toy that brings back childhood. Handmade objects sit alongside retro finds, in a cheerful, colourful setting.

And above all, they come for the welcome — the thing your reviews mention most.

Good to know

Frequently asked questions

What payment methods do you accept?

Cash, Maestro (EC card), PostFinance Card, Mastercard, Visa and American Express.

What are your opening hours?

Monday 2:00–6:30 pm, Tuesday to Friday 10:30 am–6:30 pm, Saturday 10:30 am–6:00 pm. Closed on Sunday.

Where is the shop?

At 20 Rue du Général-Dufour, 1204 Geneva, right in the city centre.

Do you sell online?

No — the shop is best experienced in person: the selection changes with each new find. Follow us on Facebook for what's new.

Can you help with gift ideas?

It's a bit of our specialty: people often come to us for THE original gift. Tell us who it's for and we'll happily point you the right way.

Do you offer handmade pieces?

Yes — part of the selection is artisanal, with handmade objects brought back from here and elsewhere.
