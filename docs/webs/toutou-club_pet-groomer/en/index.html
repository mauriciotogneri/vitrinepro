What we offer

Soft, tailored grooming

Every coat is different. So is every character. The grooming adapts to your companion — never the other way around.

Dog grooming

Bath, detangling, clipping and finishing, tailored to your dog's breed, coat and temperament.

Cat grooming

Grooming with the gentlest touch for cats: bath, detangling and coat care, calmly and without rushing.

Fragrance & finishing

The finishing touch: a light fragrance and careful finishing so your companion leaves clean, handsome and soft.

One animal at a time, by appointment. Prices depend on the animal, its coat and the chosen care — call us or message on WhatsApp for a personalised quote.

The salon

A salon on a human scale, run with heart

Toutou-Club is an independent grooming salon, run by Alexandra. No assembly line, no rush here: your companion is welcomed alone, at their own pace, in a calm and reassuring atmosphere.

Tucked along Route de Meyrin, in the Servette district, the salon welcomes dogs and cats Monday to Friday, by appointment only — to take all the time each visit needs.
